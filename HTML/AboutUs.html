About Us

Please leave us your feedback about our website!!!!

At first, Reality Club was just a group of 3 young people who wanted to save a compilation of their works. At that time, this group consisted of Fathia Izzati (vocals), Era Patigo (drums), and Mayo (bass). The vocalist, Fathia Izzati, was originally a YouTuber who often covered songs. Until Mayo invited Fathia and Era to form a band. Because she felt that the formation was lacking, Fathia invited her brother, Faiz Novascotia (guitar and vocals) and her boyfriend, Iqbal Anggakusumah (guitar) to join. Since then, the five of them have formed a music group which is currently known as Reality Club. Unfortunately, Mayo had to leave after 6 months when Reality Club was formed and was replaced by Nugi Wicaksono. Reality Club is a truly original indie music group. Because they made "home-made" songs in the guitarist's room, Faiz. In fact, they admitted that they had recorded their first album in the closet to produce the best songs like songs recorded in the studio. Uniquely, Reality Club's music is inspired by many foreign musicians, such as Arctic Monkeys, Two Doors Cinema Club, and Last Dinosaurs. Until 2017, Reality Club succeeded in releasing a debut album entitled Never Get Better, one of the songs in which became Reality Club's hit, namely the single 'Is It The Answer'. Then in 2019, Reality Club released their second album entitled What Do You Really Know?. With the target of appearing all over the world, Reality Club continues to produce cool works. Until 2019, they successfully toured at home and abroad. Most recently, they released another album entitled Reality Club Presents in May 2023. Unfortunately, Reality Club is not accompanied by guitarist Iqbal Anggakusumah on this album. The reason is, Iqbal decided to leave Reality Club at the end of 2022.
