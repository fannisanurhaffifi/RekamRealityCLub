Members of Reality Club

Fathia Izzati

Fathia Izzati Saripudin (born 26 September 1994) is an Indonesian YouTube personality, singer, songwriter and actress. Fathia is the vocalist of the music group Reality Club.

Faiz Novascotia

Faiz Novascotia Saripudin (born 14 June 1993) is an Indonesian musician and singer who is also the vocalist, guitarist and songwriter of the music group Reality Club.

Nugi Wicaksono

Nugi Wicaksono is a bass player in the band Reality Club. Nugi was not one of the people who founded the band Reality Club from the start, but he replaced the previous bass player, Mayo.

Era Patigo

Era Patigo is a reality club band drummer. Initially, this band only consisted of three people who kept a compilation of their works, and one of the three was Era Patigo.
